Things t​o Do

Thunder Road

Amusement Center

Mini Golf, Go Karts, Arcade, and Euro Bungy Jumping. Great way to kill time and have fun at the same time!

Redlin Art Center

Art Gallery/Nature Walk

Open Friday 9-5, Saturday 10-4, and Sunday 12-4.

The Redlin Art Center is an art gallery located in Watertown, South Dakota where over 150 of artist Terry Redlin's original paintings are displayed. Admission is free.

Beautiful place to walk around! We walk the paths whenever we want to get outside for a little while.

Bramble Park Zoo

Zoo

Open 10:00 am-4:00 pm daily​Open since 1912, the zoo comprises 15 acres (6.1 ha) with approximately 500 animals representing 130 different species. Admission is 10.50 + tax.

Tommy's Lanes & Lounge

Bowling Alley

Open 12am-11pm and12am-8:30pm Sunday and Tuesday​Tommy's Lanes is a 12 lane bowling alley with a full bar.
